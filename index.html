Top activities to do in Colombia

Climb your way to Monserrate

At 3,172 meters above sea level you can see Bogota from up here and visit tourist attractions.

Visit the Gautape Lake

Here you can visit the lake, take a tour or climb up the the natually forming Piedra del Penol. There are many restaurants and shops worth visiting here too.

Visit the Tayrona National Park of Santa Marta

Stay in a hotel in this national park and visit one of the most beuatiful parks. The beaches are crystal clear and the views are beautiful.
